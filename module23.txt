MODULE 23 — Voltage Profile Plots
===================================
File: module23.m (plot_voltage_profiles.m)

WHAT IT DOES
------------
Generates voltage profile figures from OPF results:
  - Per-bus voltage at each hour (heatmap, 33 buses x 24 hours)
  - Voltage at peak hour (h20) bar chart
  - Minimum voltage trajectory across hours
  - Comparison: No-ES vs ES case

Used to visualise results from Modules 5, 7, 17, or 18.
Can be called standalone or by main_run_es_research.m.

OUTPUT
------
Saves PNG figures to ./results/figures/

DEPENDS ON
----------
Precomputed res struct (output from solve_es_socp_opf_case.m)
Called with a results struct as input argument.
