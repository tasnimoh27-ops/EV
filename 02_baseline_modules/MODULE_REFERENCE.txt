================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY
================================================================================

- IEEE 33-bus feeder violates Vmin=0.95 pu at buses 17–18 and 32–33 under
  standard peak loading (Module 4/5).

- ES at partial bus coverage (P1–P4, up to 11 buses) is ALWAYS infeasible
  regardless of rho or u_min (Modules 10–14).

- Full-network ES (P5, all 32 buses) with rho≥0.60 restores feasibility
  and reduces feeder losses 18–20% vs Qg baseline (Module 15).

- MISOCP (Module 18) finds the minimum feasible ES subset — significantly
  fewer buses than P5, with interpretable placement rationale.

- Greedy heuristic (Module 17) approximates the MISOCP result with lower
  computational cost (Module 22 benchmark).
